Support & Care Center

Comprehensive guides to help you maintain, install, and care for your Displayline acrylic products. Keep your displays looking perfect for years to come.

💡 Quick Tips

Always use microfiber cloths for cleaning acrylic surfaces

Never use paper towels or rough materials that can scratch

Clean in circular motions to avoid streaking

Store displays in protective wrapping when not in use

Check mounting hardware every 6 months for security

Use only LED lights to prevent heat damage
